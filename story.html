Rainy Days | Fri, February 25, 2022

It took me 29 years to realize how lucky I am to be who I am, and not from the validation of others, but from my willingness to trust in God.

I used to get mad with previous boyfriends at how they'd look at other women while being out with me. I would let friends embarrass me when we were out because I knew it made them feel better about themselves. I let everyone step all over me and convince me that I was nothing without them. Eventually, I broke off from everybody, found myself completely alone, and began my journey to womanhood.

God was always present in my life, giving me signs about my judgment and the people in my circle. I chose not to listen and got burned pretty badly. As soon as I realized it, I apologized, confessed, and had a heart-to-heart with God, outside in the rain, under an umbrella. That was years ago and is still one of the most painful memories of my life. I think back to it when I get in late from work and stay up till 5 AM because I can't sleep.
