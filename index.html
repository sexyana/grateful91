For The Love Poetry

Poetry

There are things in life meant to be
like when you came into my world
I never thought that we would
become more than we are as friends
but you bring out the best in me
at first I didn't know what to think
we were looking for the same things
and then you gave me everything
to believe that you were all
that I need

and you're here with me
from now through eternity
i never want to turn back
to change anything
all the bad times have gone
and I've been waiting
to have found you all along
we can take on the world
being as strong, in my eyes
you are loved and you belong

There are those that never say
exactly what they're feeling
but when we are together, you show
me the meaning of a woman being
true and faithful to everything
she believes
you're the inspiration I look to
you encourage me to trust in
what could be, what will be
and just believe
