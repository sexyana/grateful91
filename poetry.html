Blessed 4 Life | 06/13/2022

yesterday, i was a treasure
and tonight, i am trash
i'll take myself out
and come back shiny.

bare moments
flaring in self-pity
the bruise will heal overnight
or hold on to what can yield
wounds to last a lifetime.

there isn't anything new
that could outweigh the pain
from things i've endured.
im used to a different
level of honesty.
i was a boss and i still am
that's the only reason
i survived.

i'm blessed to be here
and i do not apologize.

Dreams of Sunlight | 06/11/2022

life is better with dreams

i dream of sunshine
and fine chardonnay
classical music
listening to Whitney Houston
in my red robe
let down my long dark hair
the best photos of my life
framed and reflecting
sunlight from the window

and i dream of men
their energy
their smile
and sense of humor
sound of their deep voice
and silent gestures
admiring beauty
and recreation
while in my dreams
like sunlight
