Good Enough | 03/21/2022

You are a good person.
You are true to yourself.
You stand by what you believe.
We can disagree and still hangout.
You don't use me.
You don't treat me like a clown.
You never accuse me of being
Someone other than myself.
You are kind.
You have class and style.
I hide no secrets from you.
I keep the ones you tell me.
I accept you for who you are.
You make me laugh and smile.
You are someone I can talk to.
Let's be around each other
In more days than there are years.
Days add up like memories and
Life is only as good as we are.
