fri, june 17, 2022

there's a donut shop
down the street
and everybody goes there.
the drive-thru lane is short
so all the cars wait
behind each other
in the street.

do you like donuts?
i used to eat donuts.

they don't live very long
but lots of people love them
before they are made
and even after they are gone.

my favorite painting

thurs, june 16, 2022

you're the colors
on my canvas
every color is you
violet, gold, and teal
i am in love with you.
