Real | Mon, February 21, 2022

I'm suppose to be real
And I keep myself in check.
I go jogging when I feel low
Write when I need to escape
Rest when I need a break
And give my worries to God.

When I think about my future
I lay my head on the bed.
I trust in the blanket of comfort
that God puts over my head.
Everyday is a gift, but
There are days I can't shine.
I feel wrapped in emotions
And naked the entire time.

I'm suppose to be real
And check in on myself
If God didn't love me
For me
Then I'd be somebody else.

Dreams | Tues, February 15, 2022

I dream of a man
who looks into my eyes
the way the sun shines through
the windows of my highrise.
A satin, red robe
is my cape of love.
In search of life's meaning,
I dream
with Faith and God above.
As I run along the waters
and catch myself a tan,
I smile in these moments
and feel as happy as a clam.
Kissing to life everyday
with shellfish and chardonnay,
I spend time with good friends
and work smart along the way.
I dream to stand tall
in a beautiful dress,
forgiving my reflection
for all the times I felt less.
I close my eyes to imagine
how my life could be
and remember to stay grateful
for all that God gives to me.

Blessed 4 Life.
