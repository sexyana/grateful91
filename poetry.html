Love 2 Write | 02/25/2022

10 years ago
I dreamed to be an ______.
In Fashion.
My Passion.
Some things don't change.
I still love 2 write
minus pay and recognition.
Inspired by magic
I know as my intuition.
I am who I am.
But forgot who I was.
Eccentric.
Reluctant.
All.
Just.
Because.

Blessed 4 Life.
